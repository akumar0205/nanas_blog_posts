# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCE

**Date:** September 30, 2017

---

Thus by concentration during meditation we reach a
stage  where we have purged
all the turmoils eg  external sounds ,noises
etc,internal thoughts,ideas etc,images of memories and other things   and
lastly different forms and colour of core of universe.Though
they may occur occasionally we              
 observe them but are not swept
away and develop a balanced mind.We live a life with little 
or no tension.We make right judgement in our day to day life and live  accordingly.A precious gift of life.
