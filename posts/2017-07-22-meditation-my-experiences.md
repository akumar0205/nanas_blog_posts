# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES

**Date:** July 22, 2017

---

No one likes failure”it is personal funeral”. Every
action requires

effort,competency,means, their best coordination and
lastly luck.

We may have control on our effort and competency,and
console

ourself we did best to our ability.But means and their
coordination

so that no lacuna is left is not in our hand.Lastly
luck a super power

which acts in broader aspect beyond our
comprehension.It may cause

success,failure or distort our action,modify it not to
our liking.When

only fifty percent we are responsible we take
responsibility of all

factors and swing between success and failure or
happiness and

tension.So analyse failure find lacuna try to correct
it, make another

 strategy.
