# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES

**Date:** July 15, 2017

---

consciousness guides us 

In making our day to day decisions.Every living being
in universe has

its own individuality,nature,capability,means and
circumstances.One

has to take decisions  taking all these in consideration.Result may
be
