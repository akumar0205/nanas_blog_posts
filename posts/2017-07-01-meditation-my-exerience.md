# MEDITATION MY EXERIENCE

**Date:** July 01, 2017

---

Why KRISHNA is my idol.

It is said while wandering in universe Rishi NARAD
reached KRISHNA DHAM

KRISHNA was in deep meditation,when  HE opened his eyes NARAD asked  

Everyone mediates on YOU,on whome you mediate.KRISHNA
answered I

Mediate on undescribeable,unthinkable,unparallel,ultramicroscopic  devine

Consciousness.

I do not know perhaps HE might be the first
person  to say in clear words on 

Whome HE was mediating.HE knew how to communicate  with  THAT , which is

Abstract and incomprehensible.
