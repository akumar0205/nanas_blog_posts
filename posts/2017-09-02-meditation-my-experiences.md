# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES.

**Date:** September 02, 2017

---

When one has learnt technique of relaxation of
body,one should practice

relaxation of mind.

Sit in a relaxed position for five minutes,close eyes
concentrate on throb

of heart and feel it.After some practice you learn
technique of concentration.

During  practicing
concentration one listens varieties of noises,sounds,

people talking  and even you understand it, due to your concentrartion

 on heart throb
they do not disturb you one remains peaceful.

 After some practice
one may or may not listen heart throb ,even then one remains

calm in spite of various noises,sounds,talks and song .

Such persons when inter react in their day to day life
with different people

who may praise you, insult you, shout on you and disrespect
you,one listens 

and understands but is not much disturbed and takes
suitable action.
