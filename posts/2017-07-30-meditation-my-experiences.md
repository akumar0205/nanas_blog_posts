# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES

**Date:** July 30, 2017

---

In Hindu philosphy it is described as ultra
microscopic energy of brain,in hindi

we call it “man”I do not know its appropriate word in English.

Imagination,desires and thoughts are its power,they
control our sense organs

and behaviour.It is said when we need its help it
descends from brain

near our heart and we say”we are saying or doing it by
heart”.

When we practice concentration( meditation) we built
up control over

these three powers In due course it becomes our
habit.We do not remain

slave to these but become master of our
desires,imagination,thoughts

and behaviour.
