# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES

**Date:** August 26, 2017

---

Achieving balanced mind requires two things.

1.Relaxation of body.

2. Relaxation of mind.

1.Relaxation of body  
Healthy soul lives in healthy body.To develop

Healthy body we require healthy habits,healthy routine
and drug

Free life.

a.Greatest sin is tourturing ones body.One  should make oneself

most comfortable in prevailing circumstances eg dress
properly 

according to weather and surrounding.

b. Best timing is early morning on empty stomach in  a quiet atmosphere.

Though it may be practiced any time two and a half
hours after meals.

c. One should sit in a posture in which onn feels most
comfortable eg

sofa,chair,stool or ground ,head and body should be
slightly bent forward

 arms resting  on sides of body. If sitting on sofa,stool or
chair with feet

resting on ground in winter use carpet under feet.By
doing this one

gradually learns art of relaxation of body.Then
practice this before

sleep relaxation one starts getting sleep without
anxiety and on getting up

in morning one feels relaxes and fresh.
