# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES

**Date:** September 09, 2017

---

Technique remains same sitting on relaxed
position,eyes, closed

And concentrating on heart throb.

AS  we move in
concentration and learn to live with external noise

sounds,talks and music without being disturbed,we come
across

Volley of thoughts,ideas many which we never knew,some
funnys

some nonsense,some relevant and others  irrelevant.They disturb

so much we are swept away by them from our
concentration and

remain absorbed in them throughout our period of
concentration,

but when we become aware try again to concentrate on
heart beat

By constent practice they become rare and due to our
being aware

We are not absorbed in them and swept away.

Thoughts and ideas are essential in lfe. They help us
in analysing priorties,

imagning  our
aim, planning our social and day to day life.They need not

be curbed.Only aim is to choose purposeful thoughts
and ideas.It is done

 if we are aware
and not absorbed in them and not swept away.
