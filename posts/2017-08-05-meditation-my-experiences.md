# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES.

**Date:** August 05, 2017

---

Success or failure,happiness
or sorrow are part of life.We cannot avoid them.

We should
learn to live with our positive imagination,desires and thought and enjoy them.

It makes us
divine and is good for future happiness, spiritual happiness

and we get serependious experiences.

A normal disturbance
in our attitude is unavoidable but too much disturbance

so much so
it affects our rightful decisions is not accepted.We loose sight

of our
right decision.We should do what is expected from us in prevailing

circumstances
as best as possible  without caring for
its result and our happiness or sorrow.

It should
be guiding force of life.
