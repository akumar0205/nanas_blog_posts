# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES

**Date:** September 23, 2017

---

“Aatm jyoti”  is
an energy,it is core of exsistence of universe and present in different

colours and forms.We perceive these colours and its
forms in our concentration

during meditation.As it becomes more deep we perceive
starting from muddy colour of

earth to hieghest colour of sky blue.

Colour of this energy is present as muddy in
earth,crystal white in water,

red or golden in fire, green in air,and blue in sky,
as hallows expanding

and contracting (Divya Drishti a sense perception,
omnipresence of this

energy Bhagwat Geeta) As we concentrate on heart beat
and are not

absorbed or swept away,these  gradually disappear and we are left with

a blank. All junk washed away 

A blank (A black slate)A “Maan” which is under our control
---things what we

want to listen, relevant or irrlevent images which
haunt us unnecessarily ,memories
we want to dream ,omnipresence of colours and forms of core of energy.

A state of balanced mind is achieved.
