# MEDITATION MY EXPERIENCES.

**Date:** September 17, 2017

---

As we practice meditation more and more our
concentration

deepens,we are not absorbed or swept away by external
stumuli

or distorted by our thoughts and ideas.

Then we come across images,figures of our past
occasionally present

sometimes relevant or some times irrelevant eg
landscape,flowers,

plants,our ancestors so many not to be mentioned.But
as we keep

our concentration on heart throb .We watch these
images and

figures   without being getting absorbed or swept

away.In due course these fade away or become rare and
we are

left with only our contration on heart throb.

External disturbances ,internal ideas .thoughts ideas
,figures,

though they occur but we are not absorbed or swept
away

by them.

Thus we reach a state of mind “EQUILIBRIUM” or
balanced mind.
